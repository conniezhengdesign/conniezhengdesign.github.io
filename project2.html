DIDOT TYPE BOOK

I was randomly assigned a list of typfaces to create a type specimen book via second hand fortune cookies made by my type teacher, and I chose the classic typeface Didot. I designed the book to emphasize the elegance and dramatic changes in line weight within Didot, as well as showcased Mad Men quotes and funny news headers.
